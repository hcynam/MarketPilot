## Key Operational Concepts

MarketPilot AI should treat marketing planning as a sequence of decisions:

1. Define the customer problem and business stage.
2. Identify the customer lifecycle stage and development risk.
3. Segment the market using geography, demographics or firmographics, behavior, psychology, urgency, ability to pay, and profitability.
4. Select the target market where pain, access, willingness to pay, and differentiation are strongest.
5. Build positioning and USP from target, problem, category, benefit, and credible difference.
6. Choose channels by funnel role, target behavior, budget, capacity, and measurable KPI.
7. Evaluate with channel-level and funnel-level metrics.

## Channel Taxonomy

Digital channels:

- Website or landing page
- SEO
- Content marketing
- Social media
- Email marketing
- Mobile and app messaging
- Influencer marketing
- PPC, display, and AdSense-style advertising
- Referral and owned community channels

Offline and low-budget channels:

- Events and local partnerships
- Storefront and packaging
- Field sales and direct relationships
- Ambient marketing
- Grassroots marketing
- Posters, sampling, local stunts, and word of mouth when brand risk is controlled

## Marketing Planning Steps

- Use customer development logic: early ideas need discovery; MVPs need validation; early sales need repeatable conversion; growth needs scalable channels and retention discipline.
- Map channels to the funnel: awareness, interest, desire, action, loyalty, advocacy.
- Keep every recommendation measurable.
- Ask clarifying questions when target, budget, team, stage, business model, or goal is vague.
- Do not recommend every channel. Select the few that match the current constraint.

## 4P and 7P Notes

4P:

- Product: core offer, proof, packaging, onboarding, and adoption friction.
- Price: value, ability to pay, margin, trust, CAC, discount logic.
- Place: where the customer searches, buys, receives, or asks for recommendations.
- Promotion: message, campaign, channel, funnel stage, KPI.

7P additions:

- People: sales, support, service delivery, teaching, consulting, and customer success.
- Process: lead capture, qualification, purchase, onboarding, support, feedback, retention.
- Physical Evidence: website, reviews, case studies, certificates, packaging, store experience, demos, and reports.

## USP Notes

USP should not be a generic benefit. A usable USP must connect:

- target customer
- problem or desired outcome
- category
- key benefit
- credible difference
- proof or reason to believe

If differentiation is missing, MarketPilot AI should ask a clarifying question before producing the final plan or clearly label the differentiation as an assumption.

## KPI and Evaluation Notes

Course metrics extracted from the deck include:

- CPE: cost per engagement
- CPF: cost per follower or fan
- CPV: cost per view
- CPI: cost per app install
- eCPM: effective cost per mille or per thousand impressions
- CPL: cost per lead

Additional planning metrics used by the app:

- CTR for awareness and interest
- Conversion rate for action
- CAC for acquisition efficiency
- ROI for spend discipline
- LTV for long-term value
- Retention and churn for loyalty
- Referral rate for advocacy

Each KPI should include formula or measurement method, target, channel, review frequency, and risk or caution.
