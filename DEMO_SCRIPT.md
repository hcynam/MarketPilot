# MarketPilot AI — Demo Script

## Opening Sentence

*"Welcome to MarketPilot AI — an AI-assisted, course-informed marketing planning assistant. It reviews structured business inputs, asks clarifying questions when needed, and produces a 17-section marketing plan with a safe rule-based fallback."*

---

## Step-by-Step Demo Flow

### Step 1: Load the Sample Case Study
- **Click:** The **"Load Sample Case Study"** button in the form header
- **Say:** *"Let's load a sample business case — MarketPilot AI, an AI-powered investment feasibility platform targeting B2B industrial engineering firms."*
- **Result:** All 18 form fields are populated across 5 sections

### Step 2: Review Business Inputs
- **Click:** Navigate through each of the 5 form sections
  - Business Basics
  - Customer & Problem
  - Market & Competition
  - Channels & Budget
  - Pricing & Offer
- **Say:** *"The form captures the key dimensions of a business: what it is, who it serves, its competition, channels, and pricing model. Each field maps to specific marketing concepts."*
- **Result:** Fields contain realistic data for the MarketPilot AI case

### Step 3: Generate the Marketing Plan
- **Click:** **"Generate Marketing Plan"**
- **Say:** *"The built-in sample is explicitly complete, so it skips clarification. MarketPilot first creates the full internal plan, then sends only a compact digest for an optional AI strategy enhancement."*
- **Result:** The full marketing plan renders; its status clearly shows internal-only, AI-enhanced, or partially enhanced

### Step 4: Clarifying Questions or 17-Section Report
- **If questions appear:** Answer the required questions and click **"Generate final plan with answers"**
- **Say:** *"Academically, this is input diagnosis: better segmentation, positioning, channel choice, budget logic, and KPI selection require better source information."*
- **If fallback appears:** Explain that fallback is intentional reliability behavior, not a crash

### Step 5: Review the 17-Section Report
- **Click:** Expand/collapse various sections
- **Highlight:**
  - **USP card** (section 8) — shows the unique selling proposition formula
  - **7P Marketing Mix** (section 10) — Product, Price, Place, Promotion, People, Process, Physical Evidence
  - **Funnel & Customer Journey** (section 11) — AIDA + Loyalty + Advocacy
  - **Quality Score** (section 17) — 7-point checklist with pass/fail icons
- **Say:** *"All 17 sections are here, from Business Summary through to Quality Score. The collapsible design makes it easy to navigate."*

### Step 6: Explore the KPI Dashboard
- **Click:** Scroll to **"14. KPI Dashboard"**
- **Demonstrate:**
  - Toggle a KPI on/off using the checkbox
  - Edit a target value (e.g., change CTR from "2.5%" to "3.0%")
  - Edit a benchmark value
  - Click **"Reset to Recommended"** to restore defaults
- **Say:** *"The interactive KPI dashboard lets you enable or disable metrics and adjust targets and benchmarks. These are planning assumptions that should be updated with real campaign data."*

### Step 7: Copy as Markdown
- **Click:** **"Copy as Markdown"** button in the preview header
- **Say:** *"For further refinement in AI tools or document editors, click 'Copy as Markdown'. This exports all 17 sections as structured Markdown."*
- **Result:** "✓ Copied" confirmation appears briefly

### Step 8: Print / Export
- **Click:** Open browser **File → Print** (or Ctrl+P / Cmd+P)
- **Say:** *"Print mode shows only the marketing plan — no form, no hero, no controls. The KPI dashboard switches from interactive cards to a compact static table for clean print output."*
- **Note:** In print preview, verify:
  - Only the plan content appears (no hero/workflow/form)
  - All 17 sections are visible
  - KPI data is in a clean table format

---

## Closing Sentence

*"MarketPilot AI demonstrates how standard marketing frameworks — STP, USP, 7P mix, funnel strategy, channel planning, and KPI dashboards — can be implemented as a practical AI-assisted software tool. It is a strong planning starting point, not a fully autonomous marketing consultant."*

---

## Tips for Live Demo

- **Keep it under 3 minutes:** Focus on load sample → generate with zero questions → source status → KPI dashboard → export
- **For 5-minute demo:** Add more section exploration (USP, 7P Mix, Quality Score) and explain the engine architecture
- **For instructors:** The sample case (MarketPilot AI) demonstrates B2B industrial marketing context, which makes for a richer discussion of targeting and positioning
- **If AI is unavailable:** Point out the Persian fallback message and continue with the generated baseline plan
- **Portfolio framing:** Describe it as "AI-assisted, course-informed structured marketing planning with server-side Gemini integration and rule-based fallback"
